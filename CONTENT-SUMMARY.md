## Technical Considerations

### Podcast Integration
- **Hosting:** Need podcast hosting service (Buzzsprout, Libsyn, Podbean, etc.)
- **Player:** Embed audio player on episode pages
- **RSS Feed:** For distribution to platforms (Spotify, Apple, Google, etc.)
- **Analytics:** Track plays, downloads, listener demographics

### Content Management
- **CMS Options:** Consider headless CMS (Contentful, Sanity, Strapi) for articles
- **Markdown Support:** For easy article formatting
- **Media Library:** Organized storage for images/videos
- **Version Control:** Track content changes

### Email/Newsletter
- **Service:** Mailchimp, ConvertKit, or similar
- **Forms:** Email capture on multiple pages
- **Automation:** Welcome series, new episode notifications
- **Segmentation:** Different lists for different interests

### Analytics & SEO
- **Google Analytics:** Track visitor behavior
- **Search Console:** Monitor search performance
- **Meta Tags:** Proper SEO for all pages
- **Schema Markup:** Podcast, Article, Person schemas
- **Sitemap XML:** For search engines (in addition to HTML sitemap)
